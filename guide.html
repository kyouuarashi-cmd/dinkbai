How It Works

1. Getting Started & Account Setup

Before you can join the action, the club admin must create your profile. Once created, click the Claim Profile button on the home page. You will select your name from the dropdown list and set a secure, unique password.

Your request will then be sent to the admin for final approval. Once approved, you can Log In and click on My Profile to view your stats (Win Rate, Matchmaking Rating), recent match history, and upload a custom Profile Picture!

2. Joining the Queue

When you arrive at the courts and are ready to play, let an admin know! The admin will Drop Your Paddle on your behalf. You will automatically be placed into the correct queue based on your designated skill level (Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced).

3. Forming Manual Groups

Want to play with specific friends? The admin can use the Form Group feature to create a custom lobby. Groups can be formed with exactly 2 or 4 players:

  • Groups of 4: You will immediately reserve an entire court when one is available. These matches are played purely for fun—no MMR or win rate changes are recorded.
  • Groups of 2 (Competitive Duo): You and your partner will queue up together. To maintain fair matchmaking, your duo will only be paired against another duo of the same skill level, OR two solo players of the same skill level. Matches played in a competitive duo will affect your MMR and win rate!

4. The Automated Stacking System

Dink Bai features an intelligent automated stacking system designed to keep matches fair and wait times low. Here is exactly how it works:

  • Wait Time Priority: When a court becomes available, the system pulls players who have been waiting the longest.
  • Skill Separation: The system ensures that Beginners play with Beginners, and Advanced play with Advanced. If a queue lacks exactly 4 players, the system will temporarily put those players in Standby until more players of the same skill level arrive.
  • Matchups: Once 4 players are selected, the system organizes the teams automatically so that the matchup is as fair and balanced as possible based on everyone's MMR.

5. Club Rankings & MMR

At the end of every match, the admin will log the winning team. The system uses the Glicko-1 Rating Algorithm to calculate your new skill level based on your performance and rating certainty.

How the Math Works

  • All new players start in an Unranked Placement Phase for their first 10 matches.
  • Your rating consists of two parts: your MMR (Rating) and your RD (Rating Deviation, or uncertainty).
  • During your placement matches, your RD is very high, meaning your MMR will jump dramatically after every win or loss as the system tries to quickly find your true skill level.
  • What this means for you:
    • Beating a highly skilled team rewards you with a larger MMR boost.
    • The more matches you play, the lower your RD becomes, meaning your rating will stabilize and change less drastically over time.
    • If you take a long break from playing, your RD will slowly increase (temporarily disabled for now), allowing for larger rating changes when you return.
  • Dynamic Skill Tiers & Volatility: The system automatically manages your skill tier based on your current MMR (e.g., dropping below 1400 MMR downgrades you to Beginner). When you first join the club, the admin assigns you a starting skill level which dictates your starting MMR (Beginners start at 1000 MMR, Intermediates at 1500 MMR, and Advanced players at 1800 MMR). Once playing, your skill tier directly scales your MMR gains and losses:
    • Beginners: Gain and lose 25% more MMR. This helps true beginners quickly fall to a comfortable bracket and helps misplaced "smurfs" skyrocket out of the beginner pool fast.
    • Intermediate: Gain and lose MMR at the standard baseline mathematical rate.
    • Advanced: Gain and lose 25% less MMR. This ensures stability at the highest ranks and prevents massive rating inflation at the top of the leaderboard.

Keep an eye on the Club Rankings page to view the MVP leaderboard, track your rank (Bronze through Master), and review your head-to-head history against other players!

6. Dink Locker & Store

The Store & Locker is where you can truly express yourself!

  • Earn Coins: Every match you play, win or lose, rewards you with Dink Coins. Winning a match grants extra coins.
  • Buy Cosmetics: Visit the Store to spend your coins on exclusive Name Effects (like glowing or glitching text) and Profile Banners.
  • Equip in Locker: Anything you buy goes straight to your Locker. Equip it there, and everyone will see your effects globally on the leaderboards and queues!
