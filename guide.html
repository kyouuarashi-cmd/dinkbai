How It Works

1. Getting Started & Account Setup

Before you can join the action, the club admin must create your profile. Once created, click the Claim Profile button on the home page. You will select your name from the dropdown list and set a secure, unique password.

Your request will then be sent to the admin for final approval. Once approved, you can Log In, click on My Profile to view your stats (Win Rate, Matchmaking Rating), and upload a custom Profile Picture!

2. Joining the Queue

When you arrive at the courts and are ready to play, let an admin know! The admin will Drop Your Paddle on your behalf. You will automatically be placed into the correct queue based on your designated skill level (Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced).

3. Forming Manual Groups

Want to play with specific friends? You can use the Form Group button on the home page to create a custom lobby. You can form groups of exactly 2 or 4 players:

  • Groups of 4: You will immediately reserve an entire court when one is available. These matches are played purely for fun—no MMR or win rate changes are recorded.
  • Groups of 2 (Competitive Duo): You and your partner will queue up together. To maintain fair matchmaking, your duo will only be paired against another duo of the same skill level, OR two solo players of the same skill level. Matches played in a competitive duo will affect your MMR and win rate!

4. The Automated Stacking System

Dink Bai features an intelligent automated stacking system designed to keep matches fair and wait times low. When a court becomes available, the system pulls players who have been waiting the longest, ensuring balanced skill matchups. The TV display will chime and announce the player names using text-to-speech so you never miss your turn!

5. Club Rankings & MMR

At the end of every match, the admin will log the winning team. The system uses an Elo-based ranking algorithm to update your MMR based on the skill difference between the teams. Keep an eye on the Club Rankings page to view the MVP leaderboard, track your rank (Bronze through Master), and review your head-to-head history against other players!
