How It Works

1. Claiming Your Account

Before you can join the action, the club admin must create your profile. Once created, click the Claim Profile button on the home page. You will select your name from the dropdown list and set a secure 4-digit PIN. Your request will then be sent to the admin for final approval.

2. Logging In & Your Profile

After your claim is approved by an admin, you can Log In using your 4-digit PIN. Click on the My Profile button to see your stats, win rate, and MMR (Matchmaking Rating). You can also upload a custom Profile Picture to personalize your avatar across the app!

3. Dropping Your Paddle

When you arrive at the courts and are ready to play, log in and click Drop My Paddle. To ensure fair queue times and prevent remote queueing, your request will be placed in a pending list until the admin confirms your physical presence at the courts. Once approved, you are automatically placed in the correct queue based on your skill level!

4. The Queueing System

Dink Bai features an intelligent automated stacking system. When a court becomes available, the system automatically pulls 4 players from the longest-waiting skill queue. The courts display will chime and announce the player names using text-to-speech so you know exactly when and where you are playing!

5. Club Rankings & MMR

At the end of every match, the winning team is selected. The system updates your Win Rate, calculates your new MMR based on the skill difference between the teams, and logs the match in your history. Keep an eye on the Club Rankings page to see where you stand in your tier!
